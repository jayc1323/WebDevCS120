The dish in the image is Misal Pav. It is a traditional dish from Maharashtra, India. It is famous in Maharashtra and is usually had at lunch.
It consists of a thin spicy dark gravy called Misal which consists of Sprouts ,onions, red chillies. It is eaten with a Pav, which is the bread.
The dish is very spicy and people usually start sweating when they start eating it and therefore some curd is provided to give them some relief.
For people who are accustomed to this dish, some more spicy gravy called Rassa can be added. A Papad, which is a crispy item is also served with this dish.
This dish is very famous in my hometown and restaurants who serve this dish have long waiting lines on Sundays.
